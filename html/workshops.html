workshops

One thing we've greatly enjoyed contributing to Nowhere in the past and would like to increase further: workshops! If you join, we'd love to encourage you too to hold one or more. Even if – especially if – you've never done so before. Doesn't have to be anything of gravity – in fact, we love the silly ones, and don't strive for perfection.

For orientation – some workshops we've had in the past:

  • Synchronized Swimming
  • Helium Karaoke
  • DIY fetish harnesses from old bike tubes
  • Craft Your Own Banana
  • Sexy Teeth Brushing Competition
  • Proper (!) Latin for Magickal Spells
  • Development of Grammatical Gender from Proto-Indo-European into Early Latin

If you feel hesitant, let us know how we can support your idea. At a minimum, we'll offer you space in our dedicated workshop tent, and emotional hand-holding.
